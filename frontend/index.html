How Evora Thinks

Evora isn't just a scheduler; it's a smart decision engine. It constantly evaluates three critical factors: your battery's current state of charge, real-time electricity costs, and your scheduled departure time. By balancing these variables, it instantly recommends the perfect power output—ranging from a gentle, cost-saving trickle to a rapid 22 kW burst.

Under the hood, Evora utilizes a sophisticated Mamdani-type fuzzy inference engine. Instead of rigid programming, it processes data through 20 nuanced logical scenarios. Whether you have a nearly empty battery with an urgent departure, or a full battery during peak pricing hours, Evora calculates the mathematically optimal power output in milliseconds.
